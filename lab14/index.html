Lab 14: Loops

Challenge

The challenge of this lab was to experiment with loops in JavaScript.

Problems

We encountered a few challenges while working on this lab. Our main issue was getting our JavaScript to appear in our browser. Our code worked within the console, but we had to ask our friend Amanda for assistance in creating a new DOM element for each number within the loop and appending our elements to the output div. Other than that, we referred to the lab instructions and the work we did while following along with the in-class demonstration in order to complete the lab.. The in-class demonstration was very helpful in this lab and we depended heavily on the notes we took during the demonstration.

Results

In the end, we were able to list and loop through numbers 1-200. If any of the numbers were a multiple of 3, 5, or 7 we were able to replace the number with the word Fizz, Buzz, or Boom respectively.
